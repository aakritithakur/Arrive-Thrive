Welcome to Arrive&Thrive!

We help you Thrive as soon as you Arrive!

At The Airport

Clear immigration, collect your SIN, choose the most suitable Canadian SIM card, and arrange for transportation to your new home!

From The Airport

Purchase a PRESTO card to use public transit, get familiar with Canadian emergency contacts, and pick up some food after your long journey!

Almost There

Get prepared for life in Canada: learn about cost-effective and conveniently-located grocery stores, food banks, and pharmacies. Set up a Canadian bank account. Understand your student medical insurance and find a family doctor. Lastly, learn about local resources and international student communities to help combat homesickness.
