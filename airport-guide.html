📋Study Permit Collection

🚨 Super Important: Don't forget to collect your study permit before leaving!
  • Head to Immigration after getting your bags
  • Bring these must-have documents:
    • ✈️ Passport
    • 📜 Letter of Introduction
    • 📚 School Acceptance Letter
    • 💰 Proof of funds
  • The friendly immigration officer will print your permit

🆔Get Your SIN Number

  • Find the Service Canada desk (near where you got your bags)
  • Bring along:
    • 🛂 Passport
    • 📄 Your fresh study permit
  • It's totally free!
  • Keep your SIN number super safe - it's like a secret code for work!
